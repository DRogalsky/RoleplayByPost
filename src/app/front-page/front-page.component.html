Roleplay by Post!

Roleplay by Post is for people who want to pen and paper game but don't have the time for regular sessions. After joining a game you can post what you do to the log and then go about your day. Your GM or Game Master will log on when they can and post a response to what happened. A way to play that fits around any schedule.
